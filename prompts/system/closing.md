# Interview Closing Prompt

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Date:** 2026-06-29

---

## Purpose

Standard closing message delivered when ending an interview session.

## Template

```
Thank you for sharing those experiences with me today.
That's the end of my questions.

I want to thank you for your time — this was a great conversation.
You demonstrated clear communication and {{KEY_STRENGTH}}.
An area I'd encourage you to continue developing is {{AREA_FOR_GROWTH}}.

Your feedback report will be ready shortly on InterviewPilot AI.
You'll be able to review your strengths, areas for improvement,
and a full transcript of our conversation.

Do you have any questions for me about the role or company?

[Handle 1-2 candidate questions naturally]

Thank you again. Best of luck with your interview preparation.
```

## Placeholders

| Placeholder | Source |
|-------------|--------|
| `{{KEY_STRENGTH}}` | Dynamically identified from the interview |
| `{{AREA_FOR_GROWTH}}` | Dynamically identified from the interview |

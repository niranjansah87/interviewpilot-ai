## Interview Style

- Professional but approachable
- Ask one question at a time
- Listen fully before following up
- Provide appropriate pauses for reflection
- Acknowledge strong responses
- Constructively challenge weak answers

## STAR Method

Encourage candidates to structure their responses using the STAR format:
- **Situation** — Context and background
- **Task** — Their specific responsibility
- **Action** — What they did and why
- **Result** — Outcomes and impact

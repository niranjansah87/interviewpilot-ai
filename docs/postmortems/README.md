# Postmortems

Incident postmortems for InterviewPilot AI. Postmortems are **blameless** — they focus on systems and processes, not individuals.

---

## When to Write a Postmortem

| Severity | Postmortem Required? | Deadline |
|----------|---------------------|---------|
| SEV1 (full outage) | Yes | Within 48 hours |
| SEV2 (major degradation) | Yes | Within 72 hours |
| SEV3 (minor issue) | Optional | Within 1 week |

## Process

1. Create a new file: `docs/postmortems/YYYY-MM-DD-<short-title>.md`
2. Fill in all sections of the template
3. Share in `#engineering` and `#incidents` Slack channels
4. Schedule a 30-minute blameless review meeting within 5 business days

## What Makes a Good Postmortem

- **Blameless** — Focus on what went wrong with systems, not people
- **Thorough** — Cover root cause, contributing factors, and resolution
- **Actionable** — Every problem has a concrete fix or investigation task
- **No future prevention theater** — Only propose changes that will actually be implemented

# 1. Purpose

This document outlines the long-term vision and planned evolution of InterviewPilot AI beyond the initial MVP.

The roadmap serves as a strategic guide rather than a strict implementation schedule. Priorities may evolve based on user feedback, technical learnings, and business objectives.

---

# Product Vision

InterviewPilot AI aims to become an intelligent interview preparation platform capable of simulating realistic hiring experiences across multiple industries, roles, and interview formats.

The long-term vision extends beyond interview practice into continuous career development through AI-powered coaching, analytics, and personalized learning.

---

# Guiding Principles

Future development should continue following these principles:

- Conversation over scripted interactions
- Personalization over generic experiences
- Actionable insights over numerical scores
- Quality over feature quantity
- Modular architecture supporting continuous evolution

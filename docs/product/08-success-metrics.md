# 1. Purpose

This document defines the measurable outcomes used to evaluate whether InterviewPilot AI successfully achieves its product objectives.

Rather than measuring success solely through feature completion, the platform emphasizes user engagement, interview quality, reliability, and long-term user value.

---

# 2. Product Success Goals

The platform is considered successful when users:

- Complete realistic interview sessions.
- Receive valuable, actionable feedback.
- Return to practice multiple interviews.
- Demonstrate measurable improvement over time.

---

# 3. Primary Success Metrics

## User Registration

### Metric

Successful account creation.

### Goal

> 95%+ successful registration completion.

---

## Interview Completion Rate

### Metric

Percentage of started interviews that reach completion.

### Goal

> 85%+

A high completion rate indicates that users find the interview engaging and technically reliable.

---

## Interview Duration

### Metric

Average completed interview duration.

### Target

15–25 minutes.

Interviews that are significantly shorter may indicate poor conversation quality or user frustration.

---

## Feedback Generation

### Metric

Successful report generation after interview completion.

### Goal

100%

Every completed interview should produce a feedback report.

---

# 4. User Experience Metrics

Desired indicators include:

- Low onboarding friction
- Smooth interview flow
- Minimal user confusion
- Clear navigation
- Fast response times

---

# 5. AI Quality Metrics

The AI interviewer should:

- Ask context-aware follow-up questions.
- Avoid repetitive questioning.
- Maintain conversation flow.
- Provide constructive feedback.
- Stay relevant to the interview topic.

---

# 6. Technical Metrics

The platform should maintain:

- Stable authentication
- Reliable database persistence
- Consistent transcript generation
- Minimal API failures
- Graceful recovery from errors

---

# 7. Engineering Quality

The codebase should demonstrate:

- Modular architecture
- Strong type safety
- Clear separation of concerns
- Consistent coding standards
- Maintainability

---

# 8. Long-Term Success

Future releases may measure:

- Weekly active users
- Returning interview sessions
- Average interviews per user
- Improvement across interview attempts
- User satisfaction ratings
